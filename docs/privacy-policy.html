Privacy Policy - Jot Screen Recorder

Last updated: March 2026

Jot does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or recording content on external servers.

All recordings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's Origin Private File System (OPFS).

No recording data, audio, video, or usage analytics are sent to any server at any time.

License verification may occur when the extension opens. No network requests occur during active recording or processing.
